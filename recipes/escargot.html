Escargot Recipe

Description

Escargot is a traditional French dish featuring cooked land snails, typically served as an appetizer. The snails are usually prepared in their shells, which are often filled with a rich mixture of garlic, parsley, butter, and sometimes other herbs or spices. This savory filling enhances the natural, delicate flavor of the snails.

Ingredients

  • 1 can escargot, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Prepare Garlic Herb Butter
  3. Fill Escargot Shells
  4. Bake
  5. Serve
