Empanada Recipe

Description

Beef Empanadas feature a flavorful beef filling wrapped in flaky, buttery pastry.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon of vinegar
  • 1 egg, beaten

For Beef Filling:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup of olives, chopped
  • 1 hard-boiled egg, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Make the Dough
  2. Prepare the Filling
  3. Assemble Empanadas
  4. Bake
  5. Serve
