Pizza Recipe

Description

Imagine a warm, golden crust, perfectly crisp and dotted with charred bubbles, inviting you to take a bite. The aroma of rich, tangy tomato sauce mingles with fragrant basil and oregano. A luscious blanket of molten mozzarella glistens, stretching like silken threads. Toppings—vibrant bell peppers, spicy pepperoni, or earthy mushrooms—create a harmonious dance of flavors. Each bite offers a satisfying crunch, followed by a soft, warm interior and bursts of savory goodness. More than just a meal, pizza is a celebration of shared moments and simple pleasures, inviting friends and family to indulge in its delightful embrace.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

For the Sauce:

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Toppings:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 10 dollops of buffalo mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
    • 1 cup sliced tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Make the Sauce
  3. Preheat Oven
  4. Assemble Pizza
  5. Bake
  6. Serve
  7. Enjoy ***VERY IMPORTANT****
